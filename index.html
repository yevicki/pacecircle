How it works

Three steps,
quick and easy

PaceCircle keeps coordination out of your DMs and your workout on the calendar.

01 ◎

Create your circle

Set up a private group for your training crew. Invite people by link. Anyone can join, or keep it invite-only — your call.

02 ＋

Post a workout

Add a session with time, location, and type. It drops straight into your circle's feed and everyone's calendar automatically.

03 ✓

RSVP in one tap

Going, Maybe, or Can't go. Everyone sees who's in, who's on the fence, and who's sitting this one out — no group chat needed.

The RSVP system

Three states.
Total clarity.

No more "I think I can make it" ambiguity. Everyone sees the real headcount before they show up.

🟢

Going

Committed. See you there.

🔵

Maybe

Trying to make it work.

🔴

Can't go

Sitting this one out.

Features

Built for how
crews actually work

📅

Shared calendar

Every workout across every circle you're in, in one view. Filter by circle, see your RSVPs at a glance, and never double-book a session.

🔔

Activity feed

See when teammates RSVP, when new sessions are posted, and who joined your circle — without having to check a group chat.

◎

Multiple circles, no overlap

You're probably in more than one group. Your 6am run crew, your Tuesday lift group, your weekend ride. PaceCircle keeps them separate so there's no confusion about which session belongs where.


Each circle has its own feed, its own roster, and its own calendar layer. You control what you see.
